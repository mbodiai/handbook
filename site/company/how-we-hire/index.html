    How We Hire¶

    Our hiring process is designed to identify exceptional individuals who share our mission of advancing embodied intelligence and can thrive in our collaborative, research-driven environment.

    Our Hiring Philosophy¶

    What We Look For¶

    ** Intellectual Curiosity** - Passion for understanding how things work at a fundamental level - Comfort with ambiguity and complex, unsolved problems - Continuous learning mindset and adaptability

    ** Scientific Rigor** - Strong foundation in relevant technical disciplines - Experience with experimental design and hypothesis testing - Commitment to reproducible, evidence-based work

    ** Collaborative Spirit** - Ability to work effectively in interdisciplinary teams - Strong communication skills across technical and non-technical audiences - Willingness to share knowledge and learn from others

    ** Impact Orientation** - Drive to solve real-world problems with meaningful applications - Balance of theoretical understanding and practical implementation - Entrepreneurial mindset and ownership mentality

    Diversity & Inclusion¶

    We believe diverse perspectives make us stronger. We actively seek candidates from: - Different academic and professional backgrounds - Underrepresented groups in AI and technology - Various geographic and cultural contexts - Different career stages and experience levels

    Hiring Process¶

    1. Application Review¶

    Timeline: 1-2 weeks - Resume and portfolio evaluation - Assessment of alignment with role requirements - Initial screening for mission fit

    2. Initial Screening¶

    Format: 30-minute video call Focus: - Career background and motivations - Interest in embodied AI and our mission - Basic technical competency for the role - Communication skills and cultural fit

    3. Technical Assessment¶

    Format: Varies by role (see role-specific sections below) Focus: - Core technical skills relevant to the position - Problem-solving approach and methodology - Ability to explain complex concepts clearly

    4. Team Interviews¶

    Format: 2-3 interviews with different team members Focus: - Deep technical discussion and collaboration - Values alignment and cultural fit - Working style and team dynamics - Questions about our company and role

    5. Final Interview¶

    Format: Discussion with leadership team Focus: - Vision alignment and long-term goals - Leadership potential and growth trajectory - Final questions and role expectations

    6. Reference Checks¶

    • Verification of work history and performance
    • Insights into working style and collaboration
    • Confirmation of technical capabilities

    Role-Specific Processes¶

    Research Scientists¶

    Technical Assessment: Research presentation + Q&A - Present recent research work (30 minutes) - Deep technical discussion with research team - Whiteboard problem-solving session

    Key Evaluation Criteria: - Research quality and impact - Technical depth in relevant areas - Ability to communicate complex ideas - Collaboration and mentorship potential

    Software Engineers¶

    Technical Assessment: Coding interview + system design - Algorithm and data structure problems - System architecture discussion - Code review and debugging exercise

    Key Evaluation Criteria: - Programming proficiency and best practices - System design and scalability thinking - Problem-solving approach - Code quality and maintainability

    Product Managers¶

    Technical Assessment: Case study + strategy discussion - Product strategy case study - Technical requirements analysis - Customer scenario problem-solving

    Key Evaluation Criteria: - Product strategy and vision - Technical understanding and communication - Customer empathy and market insight - Cross-functional collaboration skills

    Operations & Support¶

    Technical Assessment: Role-specific scenarios - Process design and optimization - Problem-solving and troubleshooting - Communication and documentation skills

    Key Evaluation Criteria: - Operational excellence mindset - Process improvement capabilities - Attention to detail and reliability - Customer service orientation

    Interview Guidelines¶

    For Candidates¶

    Before the Interview: - Research our company, mission, and recent work - Review the job description and requirements - Prepare questions about the role and team - Practice explaining your background and motivations

    During the Interview: - Be authentic and honest about your experience - Ask thoughtful questions about our work and culture - Demonstrate your problem-solving process - Show enthusiasm for embodied AI and our mission

    After the Interview: - Send thank-you notes to your interviewers - Provide any additional information requested - Follow up on timeline and next steps

    For Interviewers¶

    Preparation: - Review candidate's resume and application materials - Prepare role-specific questions and scenarios - Coordinate with other interviewers to avoid overlap - Set up technical requirements (whiteboard, laptop, etc.)

    During the Interview: - Create a welcoming, inclusive environment - Focus on competencies relevant to the role - Give candidates time to ask questions - Take detailed notes for decision-making

    Evaluation: - Assess candidates against our hiring criteria - Provide specific, actionable feedback - Collaborate with team on final decisions - Maintain confidentiality throughout the process

    Decision Making¶

    Evaluation Criteria¶

    • Technical Competence (40%): Skills and knowledge relevant to the role
    • Cultural Fit (30%): Alignment with values and working style
    • Growth Potential (20%): Ability to learn and take on new challenges
    • Mission Alignment (10%): Passion for embodied AI and our goals

    Decision Process¶

    1. All interviewers submit written feedback
    2. Hiring team discusses candidate strengths and concerns
    3. Consensus-building on hire/no-hire decision
    4. Final approval from hiring manager and leadership
    5. Feedback provided to candidate within 48 hours

    Onboarding¶

    First Week¶

    • Company orientation and mission deep-dive
    • Team introductions and role expectations
    • Technical setup and tool access
    • Initial project assignments

    First Month¶

    • Regular check-ins with manager and buddy
    • Technical training and skill development
    • Project ramp-up and team integration
    • Feedback and adjustment discussions

    First Quarter¶

    • Performance review and goal setting
    • Expanded project responsibilities
    • Professional development planning
    • Full team integration and contribution

    Continuous Improvement¶

    We regularly review and improve our hiring process based on: - Candidate feedback and experience surveys - Hiring outcome analysis and performance correlation - Industry best practices and research - Diversity and inclusion metrics

    Our goal is to create a hiring experience that is fair, efficient, and representative of our company culture while identifying the best possible candidates for our team.

    Our hiring process reflects our values of scientific rigor, collaboration, and continuous improvement. We're committed to building a diverse, exceptional team that can advance the field of embodied AI.
